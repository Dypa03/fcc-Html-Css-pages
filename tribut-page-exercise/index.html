Link's Lore

  • The Chosen One

    Link is the warrior chosen by the godess Hylia to protect Zelda and defeat Ganon, the incarnation of evil.
    Link is also the person that reppresent the courage, one of the three parts of the triforce

  • The Master Sword

    To defeat Ganon, Link needs to find the Master Sword, a powerful weapon forged by the godess Hylia with the power of exorcise evil

  • Zelda

    Zelda is the princess of Hyrule and the reincarnation of the godess Hylia and Link's closest friend.
    Her duty is to seal Ganon to avoid the distruction of Hyrule but she can't do it alone, she needs Link's help
